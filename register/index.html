IN-PERSON REGISTRATION


Registration

In-person participants will need to sign up with this Google Forms survey.

Only one person should submit the registration form per team.

The deadline to register is midnight, three days before the contest: Wednesday, April 3, 2024 at 11:59 PM PST.

Teams registered after the deadline are not eligible for prizes but are welcome to attend depending on availability.


Practice Problems

Accounts that have correctly solved SCPE Practice Problems 1, 2, and 3 will be given priority in reserving a spot.

Make sure to submit with the same account you registered with.

After registration closes, registered accounts that have not completed the practice problems will be given a spot, depending on space availability at that time.
