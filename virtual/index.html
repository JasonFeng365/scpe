Online Participation

Although we would prefer attendees to participate in-person, online participation is open to contestants who cannot make it.

There is no registration needed for online participation.

Online participants should join using the same HackerRank link.

As the contest is the same, the online contest will take place at the same time as the in-person contest: April 6, 2024 at 10:00AM PST.

We are unable to offer prizes to online participants, as we do not have sufficient proctoring.
